Privacy Policy for the LAILA System

Last updated: August 10, 2025

1. Introduction

The LAILA system operates within the secure infrastructure of Finland’s IT Center for Science (CSC). This Privacy Policy explains how LAILA handles data in accordance with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) (EU) 2016/679 and applicable Finnish data protection laws. LAILA is a research system and is designed to work with anonymized data only.

2. Data Controller

The data controller for the LAILA system is the LAILA research team. Questions about this policy can be directed through the project’s GitHub repository at github.com/mohsaqr/LAILA or via email at mohammed.saqr@uef.fi.

3. Purpose of Processing

LAILA supports scientific research activities. Processing serves research objectives such as developing and evaluating methods, models, and tools. The system does not process data for marketing, advertising, or unrelated commercial purposes.

4. Type of Data Processed

The system stores users' email for authentication purposes. Identifiers that could directly or indirectly point to a natural person are removed before processing or analysis.

5. Interaction with Commercial AI Services

LAILA may interact with commercial AI services for research purposes. Data transmitted to such services is anonymized and stripped of information that could identify a user directly or indirectly. The identity of users is not disclosed to these services. Interactions occur only to support research tasks, and providers are not given personal data.

6. Legal Basis Under GDPR

GDPR Recital 26 clarifies that anonymized information which does not relate to an identified or identifiable natural person is not personal data. Since LAILA processes only anonymized data, the provisions requiring a lawful basis for personal data processing do not apply. If a future feature were to require personal data, this policy would be updated in advance and an appropriate legal basis would be identified and documented.

7. Data Storage and Security

All processing takes place within CSC’s secure computing environment. Technical and organizational measures such as access controls, network safeguards, logging, and monitoring are used to reduce risks of unauthorized access, alteration, or misuse. Anonymization reduces privacy risk and no personal data is intentionally stored in LAILA.

8. Data Retention

Anonymized datasets are retained only for the duration needed to meet research aims and reproducibility requirements, subject to project or funder obligations. Since LAILA does not store personal data, GDPR retention rules specific to personal data do not apply to these datasets.

9. Rights of Individuals

Rights under GDPR Articles 15–22 (such as access, rectification, erasure, restriction, portability, and objection) apply to personal data. The datasets processed by LAILA are anonymized and cannot be linked to a person, so these rights do not apply to data handled by the system. If you believe personal data has been included accidentally, contact the LAILA research team so the data can be reviewed and removed if necessary.

10. Data Sharing and Transfers

Anonymized datasets may be shared with collaborating research institutions or processed through commercial AI services for research tasks. No personal data is transferred outside the EU/EEA. Where collaborators or providers are located outside the EU/EEA, only anonymized data is shared.

11. Cookies and Telemetry

LAILA does not intentionally collect personal data through cookies or telemetry. If a web interface is used, only essential cookies required for basic functionality may be present and these do not store personal data. Any future change that introduces non-essential cookies or telemetry would be documented and, where required, consent would be requested in advance.

12. Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated to reflect changes in research practices, infrastructure, or legal requirements. Significant changes will be noted in the project documentation and version control history.

13. Contact

Questions about this Privacy Policy or LAILA’s data handling can be sent through the project’s GitHub repository at github.com/mohsaqr/LAILA or via email at your-contact-email@your-domain.
